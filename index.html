Highbridge, KY, May 2023

Highbridge - The First Cantilever Bridge

Built in 1876, Highbridge was the first Cantilever bridge in North America. It was built in the Kentucky River pallisades, carrying both passengers and freight.

The pupose of these maps was to show the natural beauty of Highbridge. The first map gives the viewer a 3D representation of the Area. The second and third show the differences in tree cover and general vegetation.

Visualizations created from lidar data provided by KyFromAbove in ArcGIS Pro. Additional sources of information from National Agriculture Imagery Program.

Page and visualizations created by Chandler Krampe for GEO 409, Department of Geography, University of Kentucky. Spring 2023.
